Constellation Planetarium — prototype

Click a name to open. Default view is the canonical "destiny shape" you see on the cert. The constellation auto-rotates slowly — drag to take over, release and it picks up where you let off. The 2D pattern is the projection along the Z axis; rotating uncloaks the depth scatter.

notes
· Each star's Z depth is derived from its own per-star seed (here mocked from the heart star's content_hash + index).
· The default view (no rotation) is identical to the 2D constellation rendered on the cert.
· Auto-rotate cadence is ~120s per revolution — cosmic pace.
· Heart star is brighter and labeled α. Other stars are β through μ in MST connection order.
